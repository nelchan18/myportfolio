Going Global

Helping Canadian Companies Navigate International Opportunities

The purpose of the Going Global Reports is to help Alberta Oil and Gas companies identify potential export markets for their technologies and services, and to provide intelligence on market access. To develop the reports, I conducted research using Evaluate Energy's robust oil and gas database and other sources. In workshops and interviews, I gathered inputs from experts and industry leaders. I also leveraged the resources, case studies and advice of sponsors who have extensive export experience.

"Alberta is an export-based economy, and expanding our markets is a key part of the Government of Alberta's plan to support economic growth and diversification. Our province's economic prosperity depends on our ability to collaborate with industry partners to attract international investments and increase market access for Alberta's products and services"

- Honourable Deron Bilous Minister of Economic Development and Trade
